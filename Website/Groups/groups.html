Mobility in Brasília

Darum geht's

Mobility is a key factor in major cities and is necessary for everyday life. Commuting to work, providing for themselves and leisure are only a couple of examples where the citizens of Brasília are confronted by challenges in their mobility. To take all these factors into perspective we took a deeper insight into the mobility system of the car-centred city. How will public transport including metros, busses and bicycles compare to the private automobile?

Governance in Brasília

Darum geht's

In the following topic, the large-scale development of the metropolitan region of the Districto Federal was studied. To find out how the Capital manages inter-municipal cooperation and the spatial development of the metropolitan region. Additionally, it was attempted to find out how cooperations within the region works and where the borders of the metropolitan region are.
